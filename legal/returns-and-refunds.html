Returns & Refunds

Last updated: 23 May 2026  ·  Applies to 3dorigins.com.au

We stand behind the quality of everything we make. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

Australian Consumer Law

Our products come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the Australian Consumer Law. This policy operates alongside — and does not limit — your statutory rights.

1. Faulty, Damaged, or Incorrect Items

Contact us within 14 days of delivery if your item arrives damaged, has a manufacturing defect, or is not what you ordered. We will offer a repair, replacement, or full refund once the fault is confirmed.

Please include in your email:

  • Your order number
  • A clear description of the issue
  • Photos showing the fault or damage

2. Custom & Made-to-Order Items

Because our products are printed specifically for you, we cannot accept change-of-mind returns on custom items once production has begun. This does not affect your rights if the item is faulty or not as described.

3. Minor Variations

Slight differences in colour, texture, layer lines, or dimensions compared to product photos or renders are inherent to the 3D printing process and are not considered defects. We always aim to match your expectations but cannot guarantee results identical to digital previews.

4. Change of Mind — Standard Items

For standard (non-custom) stocked items, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We may, at our discretion, accept a return provided the item is unused and in its original condition. Return postage is the customer's responsibility for change-of-mind returns.

5. How Refunds Work

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method within 5–10 business days of the return being approved. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a confirmed product fault.

Do not send items back without contacting us first — unrequested returns cannot be processed and may be returned to sender at your expense.

6. How to Start a Return

Email hello@3dorigins.com.au with your order number and reason for return. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

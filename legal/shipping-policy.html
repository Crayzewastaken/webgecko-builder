Shipping Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026  ·  Applies to 3dorigins.com.au

We want your order to reach you as quickly as possible. Here's everything you need to know about how we handle production and delivery.

1. Where We Ship

We ship to all states and territories within Australia. International shipping may be available on request — email us at hello@3dorigins.com.au to enquire.

2. Production Time

Most of our products are made to order. Please allow time for us to print and quality-check your item before it's dispatched.

Standard order
3–5 days
business days before dispatch
Complex / large order
5–10 days
we'll advise you by email

3. Delivery Times

After dispatch, estimated delivery times within Australia:

Standard post
3–8 days
business days after dispatch
Express post
1–3 days
business days after dispatch
Delivery times are estimates provided by our courier partners and are not guaranteed. Rural and remote areas may take longer.

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the size, weight, and destination of your order. Free shipping may apply to orders over a threshold advertised on our website at the time of purchase.

5. Tracking

Where tracking is available, we will send tracking information to your email address once your order is dispatched. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive it within 24 hours of dispatch.

6. Incorrect Addresses

Please double-check your shipping address before placing your order. We are not responsible for orders lost or delayed due to an incorrect address. If you spot an error after ordering, contact us immediately at hello@3dorigins.com.au and we'll do our best to correct it before dispatch.

7. Lost or Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged or doesn't arrive within the expected timeframe, please contact us at hello@3dorigins.com.au with your order number and photos of any damage. We'll work with you to resolve the issue in accordance with our Returns & Refunds Policy and the Australian Consumer Law.

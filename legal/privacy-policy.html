Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026  ·  Applies to 3dorigins.com.au

3D Origins is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. What We Collect

We may collect the following when you use our website or place an order:

  • Identity & contact: name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping address.
  • Order information: products purchased, customisation details, and order history.
  • Payment information: processed by our third-party payment provider — we do not store your full card details.
  • Communications: messages you send us including support enquiries or custom order requests.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs — collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use It

  • Process and fulfil your orders and handle returns or refunds.
  • Communicate order status including dispatch and delivery updates.
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send marketing emails about new products or promotions (only with your consent; unsubscribe at any time).
  • Improve our website and products through analytics.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

3. Who We Share It With

We do not sell your data. We share it only with trusted third parties required to operate our business:

  • Shipping couriers — name and address to fulfil delivery.
  • Payment processors — to handle your transaction securely.
  • Analytics & hosting providers — e.g. Google Analytics, WooCommerce/WordPress hosting.
  • Email service providers — to send order confirmations and marketing.
  • Law enforcement or regulators — if required by law.

Some third parties may be located outside Australia. Where data is transferred overseas we take reasonable steps to ensure it is protected in accordance with the APPs.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and gather analytics. These include essential session cookies (required for the store to function) and optional analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or loss. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes in this policy and to meet legal and accounting obligations — generally 7 years for financial records under Australian tax law.

7. Your Rights

Under the APPs you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Opt out of marketing at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • Make a complaint if you believe we have breached the APPs.

To exercise these rights email us at hello@3dorigins.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Complaints

If you are unsatisfied with how we handle a privacy complaint you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always at 3dorigins.com.au/privacy-policy.

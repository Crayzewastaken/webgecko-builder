Cookie Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026  ·  Applies to 3dorigins.com.au

This policy explains how 3D Origins uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep your shopping cart intact, and help us understand how people use our store.

Cookies We Use

Essential
Required for the store to work — shopping cart, login session, checkout security tokens, and CSRF protection. You cannot opt out of these without disabling the store.
Required
Analytics
Tools like Google Analytics help us understand which pages are visited, how long sessions last, and where traffic comes from. Data is aggregated and anonymous.
Optional
Marketing
If you have consented to marketing, tools such as Meta Pixel or Google Ads may place cookies to measure ad effectiveness and show relevant ads on other platforms.
Optional

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent the store and checkout from working. You can also opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

For general guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at 3dorigins.com.au/cookie-policy.

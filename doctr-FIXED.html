Frequently Asked Questions

How do online consultations work? expand_more

Our platform connects you via encrypted video link to a certified doctor. You can discuss symptoms, share photos, and receive medical advice just like an in-person visit.

Can I get a prescription online? expand_more

Yes. If clinically appropriate, our doctors can issue digital prescriptions (eScripts) which are sent via SMS or email to you or your pharmacy.

Is my medical data private? expand_more

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all video calls and adhere to strict Australian medical privacy standards (APP) and digital security protocols.

How do I book an appointment? expand_more

Simply click any 'Book Now' button, select a suitable time, and fill in your details. You'll receive a confirmation email with your session link immediately.

What are the payment options? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Some consultations may be eligible for private health insurance rebates.

Can I cancel my appointment? expand_more

Yes, cancellations are permitted up to 4 hours before the appointment for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

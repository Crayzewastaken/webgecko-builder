FAQ

Common questions

How long does it actually take? +
Most sites are delivered within 10–14 days of receiving your deposit and intake form. More complex sites with e-commerce or booking systems take up to 14 days. We'll give you a firm delivery estimate upfront.
How much does a website cost? +
Our packages start from $499 for a simple 1-page site. Multi-page sites with booking, shop, and SEO features sit in the $799–$1,499 range. We have a monthly hosting + maintenance option too. View full pricing on the Pricing page.
Do I need to supply images and content? +
No. We generate all copy and source professional stock photography for your industry. You can upload your own photos and send us your preferred wording — we'll incorporate it. But you don't have to supply anything to get started.
Will my site rank on Google? +
Every site we build includes on-page SEO: meta tags, structured data, keyword placement, Google Maps integration, and fast load times — all ranking signals. New sites typically start appearing in local search within 4–8 weeks.
What if I want changes after launch? +
Two rounds of revisions are included in every package. After that, updates are available via our maintenance plan or quoted individually. Most small changes (text, images, new section) are done within 24 hours.
Do you handle hosting and domain? +
Yes. We deploy to a fast global CDN. You can connect your own domain at no extra cost. Hosting is included in the monthly maintenance plan, or you can self-host on Vercel (free tier handles most small businesses easily).
